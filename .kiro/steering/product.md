# Product Overview

Enterprise Financial Management System (财务管理系统) for AR Company.

## Purpose
Full-stack business management platform handling:
- **Finance**: Cash flows, accounts, AR/AP, expense reimbursements, account transfers
- **HR**: Employee management, salaries, allowances, leaves, attendance
- **Assets**: Fixed assets, rentals, dormitory allocations
- **Sites**: Site management, utility bills
- **Project Management**: Requirements, tasks, milestones, time tracking
- **Reports**: Financial and HR reporting dashboards

## Target Users
- Finance teams (cash flow, settlements, budgets)
- HR teams (employee lifecycle, payroll)
- Project managers (task boards, time logs)
- System administrators (permissions, configurations)

## Business Context
- Operates on Dubai timezone (UTC+4)
- Multi-currency support
- Role-based access control with data scope isolation (all/project/group/self)
- Cloudflare Access Zero Trust authentication
- Chinese language UI with bilingual documentation
